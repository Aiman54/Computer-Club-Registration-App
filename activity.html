Activity

Activity : Artificial Intelligence


Learn the basics of natural language processing and create a simple chatbot using AI techniques. Explain the concept of natural language processing (NLP) and its role in creating chatbots. Discuss different types of chatbots and their potential uses.

Split the participants into groups of 3-4 members. Each group will brainstorm and design a chatbot concept based on a specific domain (e.g., travel, customer service, education). Discuss the chatbot's purpose, target audience, and the types of questions it should be able to handle.

Activity : Coding with Minecraft


Introduce the basics of coding through the Minecraft game and encourage creativity and problem-solving skills. Introduce a block-based coding platform such as Scratch or Blockly that integrates with Minecraft.

Explain the basic coding concepts, such as loops, conditionals, and variables, using the block-based interface. Guide the participants through simple coding exercises, such as moving characters or creating basic structures in Minecraft.

Allow participants to showcase their Minecraft creations, including coded structures, Redstone circuits, and mods. Explain their coding choices, challenges faced, and lessons learned during the activity.

Activity : Creativity with Canva


Provide a brief overview of Canva, its features, and its capabilities. Demonstrate the variety of design templates available, including those for social media, presentations, posters, and more.

Showcase examples of creative designs made with Canva to inspire participants. Guide participants in using various design tools, such as shapes, illustrations, gradients, and filters, to bring their ideas to life.

The challenge could involve creating a digital poster for an imaginary event or designing a social media campaign for a specific cause. Encourage teamwork, brainstorming, and the sharing of ideas within the group.

Activity : Learning new Tools


Start the activity by emphasizing the importance of staying updated with new tools and technologies in the rapidly evolving field of computer science. Discuss the benefits of learning new tools, such as expanding skill sets, staying relevant in the industry, and fostering creativity.

Examples of tools include programming languages, frameworks, libraries, development environments, or design tools. Organize hands-on workshops for each tool, allowing members to gain practical experience.
